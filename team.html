Dallas Mavericks Starters

Luka Dončić

Luka Dončić, a Slovenian point guard, is known for his exceptional court vision and versatility. Since his NBA debut, he has earned multiple All-Star selections and is regarded as one of the league's most dynamic players.

Kyrie Irving

Kyrie Irving, an American point guard, is famous for his exceptional ball-handling skills and clutch performances. As an NBA champion and multiple-time All-Star, he brings experience and scoring ability to the Mavericks.

Klay Thompson

Klay Thompson, one of the best shooters in the NBA, is known for his precision from behind the three-point line and strong defense. With multiple championships under his belt, he adds valuable experience to the team.

P.J. Washington

P.J. Washington is a versatile forward who can score both inside the paint and beyond the arc. His athleticism and defensive versatility make him a crucial part of the Mavericks’ frontcourt.

Dereck Lively II

Dereck Lively II is a young and promising center known for his shot-blocking and rebounding abilities. His height and athleticism provide the Mavericks with a strong presence in the paint.
